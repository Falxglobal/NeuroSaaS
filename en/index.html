02 // Identity

Identity

Ontological Definition

NeuroSaaS™ designates a distributed cognitive infrastructure, registered as an institutional artifact. This record exists to fix existence and boundaries — not to describe implementation.

NeuroSaaS™ constitutes an architecture designed to expand, rather than replace, mental capacities in high-complexity environments. It operates as a permanent structural layer of adaptive evolution, sustaining decisional coherence and structural governance.

Category Cognitive Infrastructure
Paradigm Cognitive Augmentation
Application Controlled Environments
03 // Nature

Nature

NeuroSaaS™ is an infrastructure. It does not constitute a product, consumer service, or open platform. Its application occurs in controlled environments and under a defined context.

The infrastructure is grounded in principles of metacognition, inferential ethics, and mental sovereignty, operating through intelligent friction that engages deliberate and analytical thinking (System 2), as opposed to degenerative products that reward instinctive reaction (System 1).

Asymmetry Regime

The infrastructure operates under intentional asymmetry. Its institutional value depends on context control, limitation of public surface area, and preservation of non-public layers.

This asymmetry must not be confused with exploitative cognitive asymmetry. NeuroSaaS™ implements informational symmetry with its authorized users — inferences about mental states are returned to the user themselves, combating epistemic injustice.
