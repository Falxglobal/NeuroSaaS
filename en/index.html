1. Definition

NeuroSaaS™ (Neuroscience-as-a-Service) is an architecture for the development and orchestration of digital systems based on principles of neuroscience, cognitive plasticity and metacognition — not as metaphors, but as structural foundations.

More than adaptive software, NeuroSaaS™ is a systemic cognitive infrastructure: a model for designing technological ecosystems capable of evolving in synchrony with the mental, emotional and cultural transformation of their users.

It is not a technique.
It is an operational ontology.

Final Considerations

NeuroSaaS™ proposes a model of system development aligned with the evolutionary nature of the human being.

It does not seek to replicate cognition nor automate adaptation. It establishes a paradigm in which systems evolve alongside those who use them.

It is technology that does not imitate intelligence — it evolves with it.
