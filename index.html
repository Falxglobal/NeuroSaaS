NeuroSaaS — Original Concept Term

Authorship and Public Record — FalX — 2026

Definition

NeuroSaaS is the institutionalization of applied neuroscience as the core architecture of continuous products and systems, designed to evolve in synchrony with human neurocognitive development, both individual and collective.

It is based on the structural principle that the human brain is a living, plastic and permanently unfinished system, and therefore any product that continuously interacts with people must evolve at the same pace or become cognitively limiting.

Ontological nature

NeuroSaaS is not a type of software, business model or market category. It is a neuroevolutionary function translated into product form.

Authorship and origin

The term NeuroSaaS was originally conceived and defined by FalX as a foundational concept describing systems and products whose core architecture is explicitly oriented toward continuous human neurocognitive development.
