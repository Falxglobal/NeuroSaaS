Definition

NeuroSaaS is the institutionalization of applied neuroscience as the core architecture of continuous products and systems. It is designed to evolve in synchrony with human neurocognitive development, both at the individual and collective levels.

It is based on the structural principle that the human brain is a living, plastic, and permanently unfinished system. Therefore, any product or system that continuously interacts with people must evolve at a compatible pace, or it becomes cognitively limiting.

Ontological Nature

NeuroSaaS is not a type of software, a business model, or a market category.

It represents a neuroevolutionary function translated into product and system architecture.

Authorship and Origin
